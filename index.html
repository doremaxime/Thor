Instuctions

Welcome! This website allows you to voice commands and to control the site with your hands. As a result, this site can only work if you allow it to access your webcam and microphone.

In order to make a voiced command, you must first say "please" (i.e. Please open my portfolio)

Current speaking commands:

  • What time is it?
  • What is the weather in...?
  • Open my portfolio
  • Take a picture
  • search Google for...
  • Open/close menu
  • Send email to Max
  • Calibrate

What is calibrate you may ask? Well, at the bottom right corner of the webcam you will see a red square. That is for you to place your finger or palm and say "please calibrate". What calibrate is doing behind the scenes is claculating the average pixel color in that square. It works best if your backgound is of a clear solid color or anything not resembling the pigment of your skin. Once you have clibrated, you will now be able to flip over the webcam by waving your finger/palm in that direction!

Please note that absolutely nothing, either by webcam or microphone, is being recorded!
